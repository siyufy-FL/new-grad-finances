Estimates only. PSLF requires 120 qualifying monthly payments under a qualifying repayment plan while working full-time for a qualifying employer — forgiven balance is tax-free. RAP (Repayment Assistance Plan) payments are based on AGI bracket percentages; forgiveness after 30 years may be taxable as ordinary income under current law. Standard Aggressive uses fixed 10-year amortization — no income adjustments. PSLF and RAP totals assume a 3% annual salary increase, causing payments to grow each year; Standard Repayment payments are fixed. This tool does not account for refinancing, loan consolidation, or tax liability on forgiven amounts. Always consult a certified student loan advisor or financial planner for personalized guidance.
